Peru

The Amazon with a tinge of Machu Picchu.

For a an experience closer to mother nature don't look any further than a trip situated around the jungle. Peru just so happens to home to The Amazon - the largest rainforest in the world. Before you get too worried about how much inspect repellent you will need for this 8 day trip, you won't be spending your whole time in The Amazon! You will also spend half your time conquering the other wonders Peru has to offer such as spending some time in the city of Gusco which bares uncanny resemblance to King's Landing. Oh and of course hiking your way up to the renowned Machu Picchu ruins.

Thailand

The simple hassle free holiday.

The title of one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world belongs to Thailand and there's very good reason. Boasting prestine white sandy beaches luxurious lagoons coupled with experiences with exotic animals, tigers and elephants just to name a few. Beautiful architecture such as Way Rong Khun, the "White Temple" and Bangkok's Grand Palace are even more reasons to pay this country a visist.
