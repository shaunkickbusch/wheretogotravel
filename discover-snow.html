Why the snow?

There are many thrills snowy destinations can offer. Whether it be Alaska, Canada, New Zealand, Australia or the Swiss Alps every place has unique aspects to set each other apart. However, one commonality all these places can offer is the thrill of skiing. Undoubtedly one of the most fun activites on the planet you certainly won't ever bore taking your board to the snow.

Don't fancy skiing?

The number of activites are endless. Snowshoeing, dog sleeding, snowmobiling and ice skating are more of the unique activites exclusive to snowy destinations. The Snowy Mountains region in Australia also offers other fun activites like mountain biking and bushwalking where you'll find lots of opportunities to take some memorable photos to show your friends back home.

Sightseeing

One of the worlds most beautiful natural occurances is the northern lights. The spectacular phenomenon draws travelers afar from around the world to the rugged wilderness of landscapes like Alaska and Canada. Whether or not you enjoy the snow this is one of the few sights that you cannot miss.
