Why the jungle/rainforest?

South America, Africa, Australia and Asia all hold some of the world’s most impressive rainforest holidays with untold ecologically sound accommodation and equally exciting activities to keep you wide eyed and breathless pretty much 24/7. Eye dazzling wildlife accompanied with breathtakingly beautiful fauna the rainforst and jungle is the place to be. Wildlife in Costa Rica, veggie cuisine in Peru or the amazing native Australian wildlife in the Daintree rainforest, this is your chance to immerse yourself within the earth’s living, breathing heart and lungs.

Sightseeing

The Amazon features the most amazing jungle on the planet. Gaze around for spider monkeys, take in the magical vast landscapes – it’s oceanic rivers and winding streams, its shimmering skies and the remnants of ancient civilisations. Another spectacle of the jungle and rainforest are the huge waterfalls, features carved out by Mother Nature herself. Featuring gorgeous clear prestine water accompanied with luciously green mossy rocks it is a sight that many feature and one that you don't want to miss.
